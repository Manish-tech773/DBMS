Real World Example Of DBMS

Real-world examples of DBMS include: banking systems, where databases manage customer accounts, transactions, and loan records; e-commerce platforms, like Amazon, which use databases to handle product inventories, customer data, and orders; and healthcare systems, where electronic medical records are stored and accessed through databases.
